I am Qijia He, a first-year Ph.D. student in the Department of Electrical and Computer Engineering at The Ohio State University, advised by Prof.  [Yingbin Liang](https://sites.google.com/view/yingbinliang/home). I received my bachelor degree from the Southern University of Science and Technology ([SUSTech](https://www.sustech.edu.cn/en/)), where I was advised by Prof. [Fang Kong](https://fangkongx.github.io/) and Prof. [Yifang Ma](https://www.sustech.edu.cn/en/faculties/mayifang.html). In 2023, I spent a semester as an exchange student at Stony Brook University. My research interests include bandit algorithms and reinforcement learning for large language models.

## Publication
